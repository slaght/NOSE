NOSE

Non-Objective Software Estimator

© Group 1

About

This tool is the implementation project for Group 1 of CIS 635 Software Engineering Metrics/Econ/Mgmt at Cleveland State University in Fall 2022

It aims to provide accurate estimations of software projects based on a number of metrics like COCOMO, while also weighing subjective experiences and opinions of experienced developers.
